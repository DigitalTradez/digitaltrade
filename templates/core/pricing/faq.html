There are No Stupid Questions, Ask Away, We’re All Ears

01 - How can i invest with DigitalTradez?
To make a investment you must first become a member of www.digitaltradez.com Once you are signed up, you can make your first deposit. All deposits must be made through the Members Area. You can login using the member username and password you receive when signup.
02 - What Are The Payment Processors You Accept ?
We accept following payment processors: • Bitcoin • Ethereum • Tether (USDT) • BNB Solana
03 - How Long Does It Take For My Deposit To Be Added To My Account ?
Your account will be updated after 3/3 blockchain confirmations and also when the administrators check statistics.
04 - Account ?
After I Make A Withdrawal Request, When Will The Funds Be Available On My Ecurrency Account? For your convenience, all withdrawals will be processed Instantly.
05 - Can I invest several deposits at once ?
Yes, you can make a deposit from your account balance. log into your member account, click "Deposit", select Payment System and enter amount. Then click on "Deposit" Button
06 - What is the minimum withdrawal amount ?
The minimum withdrawal amount is 10 USD
07 - Do you have any limits for an investment amount ?
The minimum investment amount is only 300 USD and the maximum amount per transaction is $1,000,000. You can contact us for customised investment plans (plans that are greater then the investment limit) .
08 - How to withdraw money ?
You need to go to Dashboard then click "withdraw". Then fill in the amount and your wallet address and then click "SEND". Our system will send money Within 24 Hours after request
09 - Do you have referral commission structure ?
Yes we do have a referral commission of 10% of the initial deposit of your downline.
10 - Do I need to make a deposit to participate in affiliate program ?
Deposit is not required to participate in affiliate program, you can earn referral commission as an ordinary member.
